Backsliding as a Believer

february 18th, 2023 by Apostle Joshua Selman

Blackslide:
Condition caused by spiritual apathy or contempt for God's things, whether on the side of a person or a community bound by a past covenantal agreement to preserve the Lord's teaching and laws.

"When I was a youngster, I spoke, thought, and reasoned as a child, When I became a man, I put the ways of childhood behind me." - 1 Corinthians 13:11 NIV. This teaches us that it is possible for a Christian to transition from a kid to an adult even spiritually.


There are many believers who, due to the reality of the times we live in, it appears that most people today view God as a necessary luggage that they have to carry on their path to finding fulfillment, on their path to becoming redeemed. Jesus was teaching and he told us that tragedies always happen when men sleep. He said that in a parable, that one came and planted wheat and then while men were sleeping, he said that the enemy came and also planted tares among the wheat.

Ephesians 5:14 KJV.

“Wherefore he saith, Awake thou that sleepest, and arise from the dead, and Christ shall give thee light.”

Many people who began their relationship with God well later discovered that they had neglected their relationship with God due to their pursuit of other things—which may not have been evil—such as education, family life, and other pursuits—until they now find themselves in a state of spiritual tragedy. In the life of a believer, it is possible to lose your fire, your vitality, and your desire.

Vital Signs That Show Spiritual Retrogression

When you encounter any or all of these indicators in your spiritual life, you should be aware that your spiritual life is in danger;

  1. A significant deviation from your love for the Lord
  2. Self-centered living as against christ-centered living
  3. Consistent ever increasing compromise of your values and godly standards
  4. Decline in your spiritual convictions
  5. A decline in your passion for the house of God
